Yeezy 500

Kanye West dropped the first Adidas Yeezy 500—a new, 90s-inspired sneaker with an Adidas Feet You Wear™ sole—last December via pre-order.

The sneaker isn't just the new sure-to-be-hyped style from the Yeezy line of sneakers, it's also one of only two Adidas Yeezys to not feature Boost technology.
